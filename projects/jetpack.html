Jetpack

Description

The most awesome-dawesome jetpack ever designed. As SOON as it's done, I'm blasting off, maaaan!

Materials

  • A SOLID cardboard box for the main body
  • Old backpack straps for the harness
  • Duct tape for reinforcement and... it's a lewk
  • Markers, stickers, and glitter glue for that razzle dazzle
  • Bicycle inner tube for flexible piping
  • Plastic cups and bendy straws for the thruster nozzles
  • Epoxy resin to hold everything together
  • Scissors
  • Two empty 2-liter soda bottles for the fuel tanks

Steps

  1. Cut and shape the cardboard box to form the jetpack's main body
  2. Create the harness by cutting and attaching the backpack straps to the top and bottom of the jetpack body, adjusting for a comfy fit.
  3. Use the bicycle inner tube to create flexible piping that connects the fuel tanks to the thruster nozzles at the bottom of the jetpack.
  4. Fashion the thruster nozzles by attaching plastic cups and bendy straws to the ends of the flexible piping, angling them downward for maxxximum thrust.
  5. Decorate the jetpack with markers, stickers, and glitter glue to make it look siiiiick.
  6. Attach the empty soda bottles securely to the sides of the jetpack body using a combination of duct tape, glue, and a lil spit for good luck.
  7. Double-check all connections and structural integrity, making sure everything is securely attached and get ready to be the coolest dude on the block.
